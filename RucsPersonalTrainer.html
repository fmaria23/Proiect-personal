Rucs Personal Trainer

Rucs is a client with whom I have had the longest collaboration; I have seen her growing and developing very nicely. With Rucs, we started the collaboration by doing some photo sessions inside the gym, but other more complex projects that are under discussion will follow.

Rucs is a fitness trainer at 18Gym in Cluj-Napoca and a nutritionist, who started this journey in 2020. She has gone through many challenges to get here; she is a strong, courageous, and motivated woman determined to reach her goals. To be honest, because of her, I started doing sports and began paying more attention to food, all of this just by having a few conversations with her to understand the message she wants to convey on her social media page.

She also told me that during school she skipped almost all her PE classes, ironically.
