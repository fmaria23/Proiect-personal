About Me

Hello, I'm Maria

I am a creative person, but also very detail-oriented. This is why I chose to work in digital marketing, as it is a very dynamic field: in some areas creativity plays a major role, while in others data analysis is essential in order to make informed decisions. I enjoy continuous learning and constantly improving my skills, and I see challenges as real opportunities for professional growth.

I create short-form videos and social media content, photograph and film various events such as conferences, fashion galas, and festivals, design flyers for social media advertising, and develop marketing and branding strategies for early-stage businesses. I manage and analyze the performance of social media accounts and websites, and I create ad campaigns that help increase brand visibility. Both my past and current experiences help me organize and structure my time and tasks more efficiently.

Since I studied photo and video at the University of Art and Design in Cluj, I have a strong background in visual and creative work. During high school, and more extensively during university, I developed and strengthened my skills using platforms that I still use today for both professional and personal purposes, such as the Adobe Creative Suite, Blender, HTML, Figma, and Microsoft Office.

I have completed various courses to improve specific skills and to learn new ones. These courses helped me better understand social media marketing, email marketing, web design, SEO, Google Ads, HTML, and JavaScript. I work comfortably with tools such as WordPress, Canva, and CapCut.

I consider myself a responsible, organized, communicative, and adaptable person. I feel comfortable in both creative and operational roles, and I work well both in a team and independently.
